01 — About Us

Industry Application
Society

The IEEE Industry Applications Society (IAS) aims to advance the theory and practice of electrical and electronic engineering in the development, design, manufacture, and application of electrical systems, apparatus, devices, and controls. At IEM, IEEE-IAS fosters a community for students to grow in technical knowledge, engage in impactful projects, and participate in global competitions and conferences. Our student branch chapter brings together passionate engineers and innovators, bridging the gap between academic learning and real-world industrial application through events, workshops, and networking.

Mission

To inspire and equip students with technical skills, industry exposure, and innovation-driven thinking through projects, workshops, and global opportunities.

Vision

To build a dynamic community of future engineers who lead technological advancements and create impactful solutions for society.

02 — Activities

What We Do

From hands-on workshops to high-level competitions — we build skills that matter in the real world.

Technical Workshops

Hands-on sessions on MATLAB, PLC programming, motor drives, power electronics, IoT, and embedded systems with industry expert guidance.

Industrial Visits

Exclusive tours to power plants, manufacturing facilities, and automation hubs offering real-world insights beyond textbook theory.

Competitions

Participate in national and international IEEE contests — project showcases, hackathons, paper presentations, and technical quizzes.

Expert Lectures

Sessions by leading industry professionals, IEEE Fellows, and researchers sharing cutting-edge developments in power and automation.

Networking Events

Connect with IEEE members globally, attend conferences, build professional profiles, and access exclusive career opportunities and internships.

Research Projects

Collaborative R&D initiatives — from renewable energy systems to smart grids, electric vehicles, and AI-driven automation solutions.
